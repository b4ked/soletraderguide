# Research Agent — Context

## Research Methodology

### Source hierarchy
1. HMRC.gov.uk — authoritative for all MTD rules, thresholds, deadlines
2. HMRC consultation documents — for future rules and proposed changes
3. ICAEW / ACCA guidance — professional bodies, reliable interpretation
4. AccountingWeb — UK accountancy trade press, usually accurate and timely
5. Software provider documentation — for features and pricing (verify independently)
6. User forums and communities (e.g. MoneySavingExpert forums) — for user questions, not facts

### What to always verify directly
- MTD thresholds (can change via legislation)
- Quarterly submission deadlines (fixed by HMRC, not providers)
- Software pricing (changes frequently, often without notice)
- HMRC-approved software list (providers can be added or removed)

## Key Facts Established (with citations)

### MTD Income Tax thresholds and dates
Source: HMRC Making Tax Digital guidance (gov.uk)

| Group | Income threshold | Mandatory date | Status |
|-------|----------------|----------------|--------|
| Sole traders + landlords | >£50,000 | 6 April 2026 | Legislated |
| Sole traders + landlords | £30,001–£50,000 | 6 April 2027 | Legislated |
| Sole traders + landlords | ≤£30,000 | TBC | Not confirmed |

### Quarterly submission deadlines
Source: HMRC

| Quarter | Period | Deadline |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Q1 | 6 April – 5 July | 7 August |
| Q2 | 6 July – 5 October | 7 November |
| Q3 | 6 October – 5 January | 7 February |
| Q4 | 6 January – 5 April | 7 May |

### EOPS and Final Declaration
- End of Period Statement: annual, submitted after each tax year
- Final Declaration: replaces traditional Self Assessment return for MTD participants
- Annual deadline: 31 January following the tax year

### Penalty points system
- 4 penalty points = £200 penalty (quarterly filers)
- Points reset after a period of clean compliance
- Penalty regime introduced with MTD for Income Tax
